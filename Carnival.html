Hamrun Carnival

The Hamrun Carnival is celebrated on Saturday morning during the Carnival celebrations on the island. It is one of the few local celebrations that is included in the official program of the Carnival weekend. ...

During the Hamrun Carnival, a handful of floats are brought from Valletta and a huge parade is done along the St. Joseph’s High Road. The floats are normally opened for the public to see in front of Parish Church and later opened again a few meters away from St. Paul’s Square.

All three band clubs of the locality, St. Joseph’s, St. Cajetan, and Immaculate Conception, take part in the parade, playing carnivalesque marches to help with create an atmosphere of fun, celebration, and festivity.
