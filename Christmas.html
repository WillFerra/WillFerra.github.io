Christmas

Christmas and Hamrun have a very special connection. This is mainly because one of the most popular local traditions of Malta, the procession with the Baby Jesus on Christmas Eve, started in Hamrun. ...

It was Saint George Preca who in 1921 decided to organise a small procession with the statue of baby Jesus on Christmas Eve. From then on, the MUSEUM Societies around the island have been organising the procession every year in every parish.

During Christmas time, the main street of Hamrun is decorated with Christmas lights and decorations. This is done to create a Christmas atmosphere for all those who pass through the main street. Apart from this, a huge modern crib is set up on the parvis of the Oratory next to the Parish Church of St. Cajetan.
