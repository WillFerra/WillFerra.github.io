Millennium Monument

This monument located in Saint Paul’s Square consists of three columns of three different sizes, one larger than the one before. In the middle, one can find a bronze map of the locality. This monument was erected as a memorial of the new millennium. The monument was sculpted in marble by Ronnie Pisani. ...

The inscription in the middle translates to: “The people of Hamrun commemorate the first full century and look forward with fate for the future. Today on the first day of the third millennium, January 1st, 2000.”

The monument was inaugurated by vice-mayor Dr. Paula Mifsud Bonnici, LL. D, and was blessed by Parish Priest Fr. Paul Fenech.
